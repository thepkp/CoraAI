# 📋 Project Requirements Document (PRD)

# Project Title

## CoraAI

### AI-Powered Digital Public Safety Intelligence Platform

---

# 1. Project Overview

CoraAI is an AI-powered Digital Public Safety Intelligence Platform designed to proactively detect, analyze, and prevent digital fraud, counterfeit currency circulation, and organized scam operations.

The platform integrates Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Graph Intelligence, Geospatial Analytics, and Multi-Agent AI to provide real-time threat intelligence for citizens, financial institutions, and law enforcement agencies.

Unlike traditional systems that begin investigations only after fraud has occurred, CoraAI aims to detect threats at an early stage and provide actionable intelligence before financial loss happens.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

India is witnessing a rapid increase in cybercrime, digital arrest scams, phishing attacks, counterfeit currency circulation, and organized fraud networks.

Current fraud detection systems are reactive, meaning investigations begin only after victims have suffered financial losses. Citizens often lack tools to verify suspicious communications, banks have limited visibility into coordinated fraud networks, and law enforcement agencies spend significant time connecting isolated cases.

There is a need for an intelligent platform capable of detecting suspicious activities, identifying hidden fraud networks, and providing real-time alerts before scams become successful.

---

# 3. Vision

To build an AI-powered Digital Public Safety Intelligence Platform that enables proactive fraud detection, predictive threat analysis, and collaborative intelligence sharing among citizens, financial institutions, and law enforcement agencies.

# 7. Existing Challenges

- Fraud is detected only after financial loss.
- Digital arrest scams are difficult to identify.
- Counterfeit currency is becoming increasingly sophisticated.

- Criminal networks operate across multiple states.

---

# 8. Proposed Solution

CoraAI introduces a Multi-Agent AI architecture where specialized AI agents analyze different types of fraud data.

The platform combines Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, Graph Intelligence, Geospatial Analytics, and Machine Learning to generate a unified fraud risk assessment.

The system provides real-time alerts, fraud intelligence dashboards, crime visualization, and explainable AI reports to support proactive decision-making.
