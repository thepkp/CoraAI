# 📖 About CoraAI

CoraAI is an **AI-powered Digital Public Safety Intelligence Platform** designed to proactively detect, analyze, and prevent digital fraud, counterfeit currency circulation, and organized scam operations.

Unlike traditional systems that begin investigations **after a victim reports fraud**, CoraAI shifts the approach from **reactive investigation** to **predictive threat neutralization** using Multi-Agent AI, Graph Intelligence, Computer Vision, and Machine Learning.

---

# 🚨 Problem Statement

Cyber fraud is rapidly increasing through:

- 📱 Digital Arrest Scams
- 🌐 Phishing Websites
- 💳 Fake Payment Requests
- 📞 Scam Phone Calls
- 💵 Counterfeit Currency
- 🕸️ Organized Fraud Networks

Current systems investigate **after the fraud has occurred**, resulting in financial losses and delayed investigations.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

CoraAI provides an integrated AI platform that:

- Detects scam messages and phishing attempts
- Identifies counterfeit currency
- Maps organized fraud networks
- Generates explainable fraud risk scores
- Visualizes crime hotspots
- Assists citizens, banks, and law enforcement with real-time intelligence
